For this game, there's one player and 3 categories with 5 clues each, worth 100-500 points. The player picks a category and point value, sees the clue, and answers in the form of a question.

Outline: 

1. Show the categories and point values

2. Player picks one

3. Show the clue

4. Player answers

5. Check if it's right, add points if correct, don't if wrong

6. Mark that clue as done

7. Repeat until all clues are answered

8. Show the final score

Since I check the answer, update the score, and mark the clue done every round, I’ll put all three into one reusable step called CheckAnswer instead of repeating several times.
